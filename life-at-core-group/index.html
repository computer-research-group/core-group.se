Life at Computer Research Group

Life at Computer Research Group isn't particularly hard — it can be fast-paced — but we try our best to keep it as stress-free as possible. Employee health is a top priority and we strongly believe there's more to life than work!

Work/life Balance

At Computer Research Group we truly believe in the importance of work/life-balance. While others use the term to sound cool, we really mean it.

Take a day or a few hours off if your friend comes to visit, go for that walk mid-day even if its a 2-hour walk. Work evenings instead of days if that's what makes your life work. It's all up to you. Eat lunch when you're hungry not because it's 12. We do not enforce a 8-17 work schedule.

Diversity & Inclusive Culture

At Computer Research Group we employ a diversity-encouraging and inclusive company culture. We don't care where you're from, who you are, or if you have any particular (dis)abilities.

Hybrid-work?

Want to work from home, a coffee shop, or even a nightclub at 1am? Go for it! Want to work from an office? We've got you covered, even if you're not based in Jönköping.

Anything else?

Have we missed something? Let us know, we're always open to new ideas and feedback. Use the contact form below or drop us an e-mail at work@core-group.se.
