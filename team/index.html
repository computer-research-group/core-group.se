The Computer Research Group Team

Ryan

Founder, CEO and Lead Architect of Computer Research Group, Ryan is an experienced computer programmer. His coding journey began back in 1998 when he bought the book The C++ Programming Language by Bjarne Stroustrup.

Ryan, who are you?

I'm probably a typical geek who enjoys anything tech-related. I've always been fascinated by technology and I remember being more interested in Microsoft Works (spreadsheets) than games when I was a kid. After I learned programming I never stopped writing code.

What do you do at Computer Research Group?

At Computer Research Group I primarily do software design, UI-prototyping, and programming. I design the architecture of our solutions. I'm also responsible for maintaining our infrastructure and I take care of the day-to-day operations. Sometimes I do presentations on subjects that are relevant to computer science.

What do you do on your free time?

On my occasional free time, I enjoy DJing, building Lego models, and taking pictures of nature/animals. You can read more about my other hobbies on my social media.
