Cloud-Native

Woodland WMS runs on servers hosted and managed by us, so you don't have to worry about costly infrastructure.

Available 24/7

We can provide nearly 24/7 access to Woodland WMS and any updates are performed in the background. No more downtime due to regular maintenance.

Worry Free

In the event the something do happen you can be assured your data is safe. We take regular back-ups of all our databases and they're stored both on-site and off-site.

Everything in Woodland WMS is journalled and if you've received confirmation that something was completed, then it was.

For example, if you're in the middle of a pick operation and your handheld computer battery dies, pick up another handheld computer and continue just where you were.

Secure

Security is very important in today's world with attackers constantly finding new innovative ways to compromise systems.

At Computer Research Group we think about security in every aspect of our operations. From early system design to development and solution deployment, security is key.

Our servers are located in physically secure and monitored facilities and all systems follow modern security best-practices. With Woodland WMS you can feel confident that you, and only you, have access to your data.
