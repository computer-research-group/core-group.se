Features

Item Management

Track all your items with an advanced item registry. Categorize and tag items as required for your specific needs.

Location management

Track all locations with ease, from aisles, shelves, and bins to pallets, carts, and crates. Never worry about a lost item again.

Multi-site

For larger logistics operations, Woodland WMS supports creating multiple sites and will automatically create per-site pick-orders from a single customer-order.

Zones

In addition to multi-site support, Woodland WMS allows a single site to be split into multiple zones. These zones can be further limited to a specific client, useful in 3PL-applications.

3PL Ready

Woodland WMS is designed from the start to accomodate the requirements of third-party logistics providers.

3PL - Customer Portal

For Third-Party Logistics providers, Woodland WMS comes with a Customer Portal as standard. Let your 3PL-customer log in and check their inventory, create orders, and more.

Integrations & API

Woodland WMS is easy to integrate with other systems like ERPs. And if that isn't enough, the Woodland WMS open API makes it easy to write your own integrations.

Localized in your language

Woodland WMS is designed to be translated into any number of languages, of which we offer English and Swedish as standard. Contact us for a quote on a specific language translation.

For management

Peace of mind.

With Woodland WMS you don't have to worry about the status of your warehouse. Woodland WMS takes care of everything. And if you still want to know, Woodland WMS can provide you with detailed reports of just about anything warehouse-related.

For users

Ease of use.

Woodland WMS makes it easy for any one working on the floor of a warehouse to get their tasks done, and on time. No hassles with counter-intuitive user interfaces, no cryptic error messages, no upgrades in the middle of a workday. It. Just. Works.
