History

The Fridge

Many years ago, our founder Ryan, came in contact with a WMS-like system used by a large retail chain in Sweden. Instantly fascinated by the handheld device with a built-in barcode scanner, Ryan wanted to have a similar system at home to keep track of the items in his fridge.

Said and done, Stockroom was born. A very simple system, but it worked.

After having implemented Stockroom and working with some other big warehouse management systems professionally, Ryan realized a great potential to implement a really user-friendly WMS.

— Most of the systems I came in contact with were either huge and difficult to set up, or too small to grow with. User friendliness wasn't a top priority either, says Ryan.

Woodland Wms

That's how the idea for Woodland WMS was beginning to grow. And here we are a few years later.

What's you ambition for Woodland WMS?

— I want Woodland WMS to be the go-to choice for anyone looking for a WMS. From the small e-commerce start-up to the larger 3PL-player, Woodland WMS will provide the services required.

Trivia

Did you know? Woodland WMS is named after Norman Joseph Woodland, best known as co-inventor of the barcode.
