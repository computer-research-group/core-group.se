Service Offering

Computer Research Group offers several services to bring your ideas to life!

Software Development

From a napkin sketch via prototype to a production-ready system, we can help you realize your software ideas.

At Computer Research Group, we specialize in development using C++, C#/.NET and TypeScript. We can develop server applications running on Linux, native applications for both Windows and Linux, or Android and iOS apps.

Do you need something else? Just contact us! We've done development for more obscure systems like EPOC and BeOS in the past, we're fast learners!

Hosting

Don't want to have to worry about managing infrastructure or daily backups? We've got you covered! Let us handle everything from software installation to daily maintenance and backups.

We have several years of experience running mission-critical systems on Microsoft Azure, AWS, and DigitalOcean. Using tools such as Docker, Terraform, and Ansible to manage the IT-environment. Don't hesitate to contact us and we can discuss your needs and how we can help.

First Class Support

We offer first-class office-hours support for all our commercially sold solutions and services. In addition it's possible to sign service-level agreements for our open-soure projects as well. Just give us a call and we'll help you!

Training

To maximize the benefit of using Computer Research Group solutions, let us train your employees. We can offer on-site workshops or put together custom-made online courses, all depending on your needs.

Just contact us today to get a quote!
